**Golden Harvest** is a warm and earthy color palette inspired by the wheat belt during summer. The palette captures the transformation of wheat as it's harvested, with rich earthy tones and also, subtle accents drawn from the Nord color palette.

## Palette Breakdown

### 1. **Dark Foundations**
Deep, earthy colors inspired by soil, bark, and clay.
- **Dark Soil** (#40352A)
- **Deep Bark** (#584939)
- **Weathered Wood** (#6A5845)
- **Harvest Bark** (#79644F)
- **Dry Clay** (#8B725C)

### 2. **Warm Earth**
Golden and sun-soaked hues of ripening wheat.
- **Autumn Soil** (#9B8066)
- **Golden Stubble** (#AC8E71)
- **Wheat** (#d6c09a)
- **Ripe Wheat** (#EBCB8B)

### 3. **Accents**
Subtle, cool tones from the Nord palette, offering contrast.
- **Cool Sky** (#81A1C1)
- **Morning Dew** (#8FBCBB)
- **Fresh Sprout** (#A3BE8C)
- **Berry Rust** (#BF616A)
- **Magenta** (#b48ead)
